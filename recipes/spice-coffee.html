Arrakeen Spice Coffee

From Dune

A simple recipe for making Dune inspired Arrakeen Spice Coffee

Ingrediants

  • Coffee
  • 1 tsp cinnamon or more
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Creamer (optional) Vanilla is good

Steps

  1. Set up your coffee pot.
  2. Make the coffee as strong as you can stand.
  3. Before you brew the coffee, place cinnamon into the grounds. Brew coffee as usual.
  4. Sweeten or add cream to taste.
  5. Sip coffee, do not chug.
  6. This coffee will have a good strong cinnamon flavor, however, take care not to add too much cinnamon because it can clog your coffee filter resulting in hot water and grounds everywhere.
  7. Cinnamon is not water soluable so keep it to 1-2 teaspoons.

Recipe from The Experimental Chef
